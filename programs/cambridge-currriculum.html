Advanced Level (A Level) - REB Curriculum

Three-Year Advanced Program (S4 - S6)

Our Advanced Level program is designed to prepare students for university entrance and professional careers through rigorous REB-based curriculum. The program spans three years with specialized pathways in Science, Mathematics, Humanities, Languages, and specialized combinations.

Senior 4 (S4) - Foundation Year

Students choose one of four specialized streams to establish their academic foundation:

  • MS1 - Maths and Sciences One
  • MS2 - Maths and Sciences Two
  • ARH - Art and Humanities
  • LGG - Languages

Senior 5 & 6 (S5-S6) - Advanced Specialization

Students advance to specialized combinations based on their career aspirations and academic performance:

  • PCM - Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (Engineering/Science)
  • MPG - Mathematics, Physics, Geography (Sciences)
  • MEG - Mathematics, Economics, Geography (Social Sciences)
  • PCB - Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Medical Sciences)
  • HGL - History, Geography, Literature (Humanities)
  • LFK - Languages, French, Kinyarwanda (Languages)
  • MCB - Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology (Applied Sciences)

All programs follow the REB (Rwanda Education Board) curriculum framework, ensuring quality education aligned with national and international standards.
