Admissions at Rusumo High School

Quality education through a transparent and fair admission process

How We Admit Students

Rusumo High School admits students through a transparent and merit-based process managed by the Rwanda Education Board (REB) and the National Examination and School Placement (NESA) system.

Admission Sources

1. National Placement (NESA/REB)

Students are placed in Rusumo High School based on:

  • National examination results
  • Student preference and school ranking
  • NESA placement algorithm
  • Availability of spaces in each program

Note: This is the primary admission method for most students. The placement process is conducted by the Rwanda Education Board and NESA.

2. Transfer Students

Students may transfer to Rusumo High School based on:

  • Approval from the Rwanda Education Board (REB)
  • Availability of spaces in the requested program
  • Academic eligibility
  • Compliance with school policies

Process: Contact the administration office for transfer procedures and requirements. All transfers require REB authorization.

Key Principle

External Regulation: Admission is externally regulated by the Rwanda Education Board (REB) and NESA. Rusumo High School does not conduct internal selection for primary admissions. Our role is to ensure spaces are available and to support students once admitted.

Programs Offered for O and A Levels

Ordinary Level (S1-S3): General curriculum with focus on foundational academics, character development, and exploration of interests.

Advanced Level (S4-S6): Specialized programs with the following streams available:

S4 Stream Options
  • MS1 - Maths & Sciences One
  • MS2 - Maths & Sciences Two
  • ARH - Art & Humanities
  • LGG - Languages
S5-S6 Combinations
  • PCM, PCB, MCB, MPG
  • MEG, HGL, LFK

Boarding System

Rusumo High School is a boarding school committed to providing a supportive and disciplined environment that complements academic excellence.

  • Safe residential facilities: Modern dormitories for male and female students
  • 24/7 supervision: Trained residential staff ensures student welfare
  • Study support: Structured study hours and tutorial sessions
  • Pastoral care: Guidance and counseling services available
  • Community living: Fosters teamwork, discipline, and mutual respect

Ready to Apply?

If you have been placed at Rusumo High School through NESA or wish to inquire about transfer opportunities, we're ready to welcome you!

Next Steps:

  1. Verify your placement through the official NESA platform
  2. Contact our administration office with your placement letter
  3. Complete registration formalities
  4. Report on the official reporting date
