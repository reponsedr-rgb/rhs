School Facilities

Science Laboratory

Our sciences lab is fully equipped with modern equipment and supplies to facilitate practical learning experiences for our students. We have a dedicated team of science teachers who guide students through various experiments and activities, helping them to gain a deeper understanding of scientific concepts and principles.

View details

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is a state-of-the-art facility that is equipped with modern computer hardware and software. It provides our students with hands-on experience in the latest technologies and tools, enabling them to develop their computer literacy skills. Our computer lab is supervised by trained technicians who ensure that our equipment is always in good working condition

View details

Library

Our library is a spacious and modern facility that is designed to provide our students with a quiet and comfortable space for reading, research, and study. It is equipped with a wide range of books, periodicals, and digital resources to support learning and research in various fields. Our library is staffed by qualified librarians who are always ready to assist students with their research needs.

Sports Fields

Modern sports facilities including football pitch, basketball court, and volleyball court. Provides comprehensive physical education and competitive sports opportunities for students.

Dormitories

Safe and comfortable residential facilities for our boarding students. Well-maintained dormitories with 24/7 supervision and pastoral care.

Dining Hall

Spacious dining facility serving nutritious meals to all students. Hygienic food preparation and serving standards maintained.

Smart Classrooms

Modern equipped classrooms with interactive boards and technology integration for enhanced learning experiences.
