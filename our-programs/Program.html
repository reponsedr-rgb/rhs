Our Programs

Ordinary Level (O Level)

A comprehensive REB-based curriculum designed to provide students with essential academic knowledge and skills. Focuses on building strong foundations in core and elective subjects for future advanced studies.

View details

Advanced Level (A Level)

Three-year specialized program (S4-S6) offering diverse subject combinations: MS1, MS2, ARH, LGG in S4, and PCM, MPG, MEG, PCB, HGL, LFK, MCB in S5-S6. Rigorous REB-based curriculum for academic excellence and critical thinking.
