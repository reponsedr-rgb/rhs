Welcome to RHS

Rusumo High School (RHS) is located in Kigina Sector, Kirehe District, Rwanda. Rusumo High School is a registered school with the Ministry of Education. It is committed to providing quality education and excellence. school and throughout these sixteen years, it has been performing exceptionally well at the national level. The success of the O' level section paves way for the Advanced Level (A level) with comprehensive combinations. Our Advanced Level programs (S4-S6) include: S4 offers MS1 (Maths and Sciences One), MS2 (Maths and Sciences Two), ARH (Art and Humanities), and LGG (Languages). S5-S6 students can choose from PCM, MPG, MEG, PCB, HGL, LFK, and MCB combinations. All programs are based on the REB (Rwanda Education Board) curriculum, ensuring quality education aligned with national standards.

Our Programs

Ordinary Level (O Level)

A comprehensive REB-based curriculum designed to provide students with essential academic knowledge and skills. Focuses on building strong foundations in core and elective subjects for future advanced studies.

Advanced Level (A Level)

Three-year specialized program (S4-S6) offering diverse subject combinations: MS1, MS2, ARH, LGG in S4, and PCM, MPG, MEG, PCB, HGL, LFK, MCB in S5-S6. Rigorous REB-based curriculum for academic excellence and critical thinking.
