School background

Rusumo High School (RHS) is located in Kigina Sector, Kirehe District, Rwanda. RHS is a registered school with the ministry of Education. Dedicated to fostering excellence in character, academics, and service to the nation.

The O' level section was ranked among the best school in the national levels. The incredible success of the O' level section paves way for the Advanced Level (A level) with comprehensive combinations. Our Advanced Level programs (S4-S6) include: S4 offers MS1 (Maths and Sciences One), MS2 (Maths and Sciences Two), ARH (Art and Humanities), and LGG (Languages). S5-S6 students can choose from PCM, MPG, MEG, PCB, HGL, LFK, and MCB combinations. All programs are based on the REB (Rwanda Education Board) curriculum, ensuring quality education aligned with national standards.
