Student Life & Activities

Building Tomorrow's Leaders Through Engagement and Excellence

Clubs & Societies

Rusumo High School offers various clubs and societies that provide students with opportunities to develop their talents, interests, and leadership skills.

Debate Club

Develop public speaking and critical thinking skills through competitive debating and discussion forums.

Science Club

Explore scientific discoveries, conduct experiments, and participate in science competitions and projects.

Entrepreneurship Club

Learn business skills, develop entrepreneurial mindset, and create innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Public Speaking Club

Build confidence and communication skills through presentations, seminars, and public speaking events.

Sports & Athletics

Our comprehensive sports program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and competitive excellence.

Football

Competitive football teams with regular training, inter-house tournaments, and national championships.

Basketball

Dynamic basketball program with dedicated training, friendly matches, and competitive leagues.

Volleyball

Organized volleyball training and competitions for both male and female students.

Cultural Activities

Celebrating diversity and cultural heritage through various activities and events.

Debate Competitions

Participate in debate competitions at school, regional, and national levels to showcase critical thinking and oratory skills.

Entrepreneurship Challenges

Organize business plan competitions and entrepreneurship challenges that foster innovation and business acumen.

Academic Competitions

Competitions in various subjects to celebrate academic excellence and encourage scholarly pursuits.

Sports Championships

Annual inter-house and national sports championships where students compete for glory and school pride.

Student Development Programs

Leadership Training

Develop leadership qualities through workshops, mentorship programs, and student leadership roles.

Character Development

Programs focused on building integrity, discipline, and moral values aligned with our school motto.

Life Skills

Training in communication, time management, conflict resolution, and other essential life skills.
